Quick Accessibility Controls

Accessibility at HomeHub

We're committed to making HomeHub accessible to everyone. Our platform follows WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and includes features to support users with diverse abilities and preferences.

Accessibility Features

Full Keyboard Navigation

Navigate the entire interface using just your keyboard. All interactive elements are reachable and operable without a mouse.

Screen Reader Support

Optimized for popular screen readers with proper ARIA labels, semantic HTML, and descriptive alternative text for images.

Customizable Themes

Choose from light, dark, and high contrast themes. Adjust colors to reduce eye strain and improve visibility.

Adjustable Text Size

Increase or decrease text size to your preference. The layout adapts to ensure content remains readable and accessible.

Audio Descriptions

Audio descriptions available for visual content, ensuring information is accessible to users with visual impairments.

Reduced Motion Support

Respects prefers-reduced-motion settings to minimize animations for users who experience vestibular motion disorders.
