Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.

Property listings are typically reviewed and approved within 24 hours. During peak times, this may take up to 48 hours. You'll receive a notification once your property is live on the platform.

You can contact your landlord or tenant through the messaging system in your HomeHub dashboard. Simply go to your properties or rentals section, select the property, and click on the "Message" button.

HomeHub accepts various payment methods including M-Pesa, bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. The available options may vary depending on your location and the type of transaction.

If you're experiencing issues with your rental, you can submit a maintenance request through your dashboard. For urgent issues, contact your landlord directly through the messaging system or call our support team.

Service providers get paid through the platform once a job is completed and approved by the property owner. Payments are typically processed within 24-48 hours after completion and can be withdrawn to your bank account or mobile money wallet.
