Our Story

Why HomeHub Was Created

Property management in Kenya has long been plagued by challenges: late rent payments, communication gaps between landlords and tenants, inefficient maintenance processes, and poor record-keeping. These issues created frustration for property owners, tenants, and service providers alike.

Recognizing these pain points, our team of real estate professionals and technology experts came together to create HomeHub - a platform that would address these challenges head-on and transform the property management landscape.

How HomeHub Solves These Problems

  • Automated Rent Collection: Timely payments with M-Pesa integration
  • Centralized Communication: Direct messaging between all parties
  • Efficient Maintenance: Quick connection to verified service providers
  • Digital Record-Keeping: Secure storage of leases, payments, and documents
